River Runners

River Runners is a single player auto-scroller adventure game developed for PC. The player plays against the rapid currents of the river while controlling a log and a merry band of animals, attempting to avoid the various obstacles that appear along the way.

Play Here

This project was developed in Godot, with GDScript in a span of 4 months for a master's degree course, a project in 2D, with 3 colleagues.
In this project I developed:

  • Gameplay mechanics, like the player movement, and character abilities
  • UI elements, like the main menus, settings, and in-game elements like the progress bar, health, and characters abilities cooldowns
  • Animations, for the characters and obstacles
This game was selected for various events like:
  • Aveiro Jovem Criador 2024 - https://www.cm-aveiro.pt/municipio/comunicacao/noticias/noticia/aveiro-jovem-criador-2024-revelou-vencedores-e-inaugurou-exposicao-no-museu-de-aveiro-santa-joana - participated and the game was exposed in the museum.
  • Novos Talentos FNAC 2024 https://www.fnac.pt/novostalentos - just participated
  • Media Play UA 2024 - https://mediaplay.web.ua.pt/ - finished 3rd place in the public voting.

Parry&Strike

Parry & Strike is a 2D strategic serious game designed to teach players the fundamental principles and poses of longsword dueling in HEMA, the Historic European Martial Arts. The players will assume the role of duelists, challenging a bot, using skills representing longsword attacks and defensive moves.

Play Here

This project was developed in Unity, with C# in 1 month for a master's degree course, a serious game.
I programmed the game but the art comes from free assets on the internet.
